About Githure Secondary

Discover our story, mission, and values

Our History

Founded in 1985, Githure Secondary School has been a beacon of educational excellence in the region for over four decades. What started as a small community school has grown into a premier secondary institution serving students from across the county.

Through the years, we have maintained our commitment to holistic education, producing graduates who excel in academics, leadership, and character. Our alumni include doctors, engineers, teachers, and community leaders who continue to make positive contributions to society.

🏫 School Photo

Our Mission

To provide quality, holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, moral integrity, and responsible citizenship in a supportive and stimulating environment.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence producing well-rounded, innovative, and morally upright leaders who transform their communities.
