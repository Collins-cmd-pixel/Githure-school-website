Form 1 Entry Requirements

  • 📄 KCPE results slip (original and copy)
  • 📝 Birth certificate (copy)
  • 🖼️ 2 passport size photos
  • 🏥 Medical history form
  • 👕 Recommendation from primary school
  • 📋 Completed application form

Form 2-4 Transfers

  • 📚 Previous school reports (last 2 terms)
  • 📄 Leaving certificate/transfer letter
  • 📝 Birth certificate (copy)
  • 🖼️ 2 passport size photos
  • 🏥 Medical history form
  • 💵 Fee payment receipt (if applicable)
