Student Life

⚽ Sports & Athletics

⚽

Football

Boys and girls teams competing at sub-county and county levels. Training every Tuesday and Thursday.

🏐

Volleyball

Both indoor and beach volleyball teams. Regular friendly matches with neighboring schools.

🏃

Athletics

Track and field events including sprints, middle distance, relays, and field events.

🏀

Basketball

Growing basketball program with weekly training sessions and tournaments.
