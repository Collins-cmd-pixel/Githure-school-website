Frequently Asked Questions

📌 What are the admission requirements?
For Form 1 entry, you need KCPE results, birth certificate, and 2 passport photos. Transfer students need previous school reports. Visit our Admissions page for full details.
📌 When does the school year start?
The academic year follows the national school calendar. Term 1 typically begins in January, Term 2 in May, and Term 3 in September.
📌 Do you offer boarding facilities?
Yes, we have well-maintained boarding facilities for both boys and girls with house masters/mistresses and full-time matrons.
📌 How can parents track student progress?
We hold regular parent-teacher conferences, send home progress reports every term, and have a parent portal for online access.
